Disclosure - Favorites

Get to know my favorite Disclosure Albums and listen them on Spotify.

Settle

ettle is the debut studio album by English electronic music duo Disclosure.It was released on 31 May 2013 by PMR Records and Island Records, Mercury Prize Winners and worldwide recognition followed.

Carcal

On the follow-up to their massive 2013 hit Settle, the brothers Guy and Howard Lawrence have dialed down the BPMs significantly and turned toward slow-burning, R&B-inspired grooves.

Ecstasy EP

Ecstasy is the first collection of music released by brothers Howard and Guy Lawrence since 2016’s. Earlier this month, the brothers teamed up with Khalid for his track “Know Your Worth.”
